Arian Shariat is an Iranian musician, singer-songwriter, and producer with a background in Computer Engineering. His professional music journey began with Jetpack in 2017, and has since expanded into the solo project Rian, the Persian alternative/fusion band Jilliz Williz, and different collaborations as a songwriter and producer.
